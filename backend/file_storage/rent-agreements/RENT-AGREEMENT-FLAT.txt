RENT AGREEMENT

This Rent Agreement is made and executed at ____ on this ____ between:

LANDLORD:
____, Son/Daughter/Wife of ____, residing at ____, hereinafter referred to as the "LANDLORD" (which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, include his/her heirs, successors, legal representatives and assigns) of the FIRST PART;

AND

TENANT:
____, Son/Daughter/Wife of ____, residing at ____, hereinafter referred to as the "TENANT" (which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, include his/her heirs, successors, legal representatives and assigns) of the SECOND PART.

WHEREAS the Landlord is the absolute owner of the residential flat/premises situated at ____, described as ____, and the Tenant has approached the Landlord for taking the said premises on rent for residential purposes, to which the Landlord has agreed on the following terms and conditions:

NOW THIS AGREEMENT WITNESSETH AS FOLLOWS:

1. TERM OF TENANCY: The tenancy shall commence from ____ and shall remain valid until ____, unless terminated earlier by either party as per the terms of this agreement.

2. MONTHLY RENT: The Tenant shall pay a monthly rent of Rs. ____ (Rupees ______ only) to the Landlord. The rent shall be payable on or before the ____ day of each English calendar month.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT: The Tenant has paid a sum of Rs. ____ (Rupees ______ only) as security deposit to the Landlord at the time of execution of this agreement. This deposit shall be refundable without interest at the time of vacating the premises, after deducting any outstanding rent, utility charges, or damages to the property.

4. UTILITY CHARGES: The Tenant shall bear and pay all charges for electricity, water, gas, and any other utility services consumed during the tenancy period. The Tenant shall ensure timely payment of all such bills.

5. MAINTENANCE: The Tenant shall maintain the premises in good and habitable condition. Any minor repairs required due to normal wear and tear shall be borne by the Landlord. Any damage caused by negligence of the Tenant shall be repaired at the Tenant's cost.

6. SUB-LETTING: The Tenant shall not sub-let, assign, or transfer the tenancy or any part thereof to any third party without prior written consent of the Landlord.

7. PURPOSE: The premises shall be used solely for residential purposes. The Tenant shall not use the premises for any illegal, immoral, or commercial activity.

8. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this agreement by giving ____ month(s) written notice to the other party. In case of breach of any terms, the Landlord may terminate the agreement immediately.

9. RETURN OF POSSESSION: Upon expiry or termination of this agreement, the Tenant shall vacate the premises peacefully and hand over vacant possession to the Landlord in the same condition as received, subject to normal wear and tear.

10. GENERAL: Both parties agree to abide by the provisions of the applicable Rent Control Act and other laws in force. Any dispute arising out of this agreement shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts at the place of execution.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have set their hands on this agreement on the date, month, and year first above written.
