HOUSE RENT AGREEMENT

This Rent Agreement is made and executed at ____ on this ____ between:

LANDLORD:
____, Son/Daughter/Wife of ____, residing at ____, hereinafter referred to as the "LANDLORD" (which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, include his/her heirs, successors, legal representatives and assigns) of the FIRST PART;

AND

TENANT:
____, Son/Daughter/Wife of ____, residing at ____, hereinafter referred to as the "TENANT" (which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, include his/her heirs, successors, legal representatives and assigns) of the SECOND PART.

WHEREAS the Landlord is the absolute owner of the residential house/property situated at ____, described as ____, and the Tenant has approached the Landlord for taking the said house on rent for residential purposes, to which the Landlord has agreed on the following terms and conditions:

NOW THIS AGREEMENT WITNESSETH AS FOLLOWS:

1. TERM OF TENANCY: The tenancy shall commence from ____ and shall remain valid until ____, unless terminated earlier by either party as per the terms of this agreement.

2. MONTHLY RENT: The Tenant shall pay a monthly rent of Rs. ____ (Rupees ______ only) to the Landlord.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT: The Tenant has paid a sum of Rs. ____ (Rupees ______ only) as security deposit to the Landlord. This deposit shall be refundable without interest upon vacating the premises, after deducting any outstanding dues or damages.

4. UTILITY CHARGES: The Tenant shall bear and pay all charges for electricity, water, gas, and any other utility services consumed during the tenancy period.

5. MAINTENANCE OF PROPERTY: The Tenant shall maintain the house, garden, compound, and all fixtures in good condition. Structural repairs and major maintenance shall be the Landlord's responsibility. Any damage due to Tenant's negligence shall be repaired at Tenant's cost.

6. ALTERATIONS: The Tenant shall not make any structural changes, additions, or alterations to the property without prior written consent of the Landlord.

7. SUB-LETTING: The Tenant shall not sub-let, assign, or transfer the tenancy or any part thereof to any third party without prior written consent of the Landlord.

8. PURPOSE: The premises shall be used solely for residential purposes. The Tenant shall not carry on any business, trade, or commercial activity from the premises.

9. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this agreement by giving ____ month(s) written notice to the other party. In case of breach, the Landlord may terminate immediately.

10. RETURN OF POSSESSION: Upon expiry or termination, the Tenant shall vacate the house peacefully and return vacant possession to the Landlord in the same condition as received, subject to normal wear and tear.

11. GENERAL: This agreement is subject to the applicable Rent Control Act and other laws in force. Any dispute shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts at the place of execution.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have set their hands on this agreement on the date, month, and year first above written.
